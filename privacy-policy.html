Summary: Scriptly is built with your privacy in mind. All your recordings and notes are stored exclusively on your device — never on our servers. We do not sell your data, we do not use your audio to train AI models, and we do not access your microphone in the background.

Section 01

Who We Are

This Privacy Policy describes how Scriptly ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares your personal information when you use the Scriptly mobile application (the "App") on iOS and Android devices.

By using Scriptly, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use the App.

Contact: For any privacy-related questions, reach us at supp.scriptly@gmail.com

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we are the data controller responsible for your personal information under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Section 02

Information We Collect

2.1 Audio and Voice Data

Scriptly accesses your device's microphone when you initiate a recording session. We collect:

  • Live audio input: Raw audio captured through your microphone during speech-to-text sessions, processed to generate text transcriptions.
  • Uploaded audio and video files: MP3 and MP4 files you voluntarily import into the App for transcription.

Important: Scriptly does not record audio in the background. Microphone access is active only when you explicitly start a recording within the App. We do not build a biometric voice profile or use your voice for speaker identification.

2.2 Transcriptions and Notes

We collect the text content of your transcriptions, notes, folder names, and any edits you make — including the results of AI-powered filler word removal. This content is yours.

2.3 Device and Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information to maintain App stability, including device model, OS version, App version, preferred language, crash reports, and diagnostic logs. This data is stripped of any content from your recordings or notes.

2.4 Account Information

Scriptly does not require account creation. No account data is collected.

2.5 What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect government IDs, Social Security numbers, or financial account information.
  • We do not perform facial recognition or build biometric voice profiles.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, location, or any device data beyond what is listed above.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
Section 03

How We Use Your Information

3.1 Core App Features

  • Speech-to-Text Transcription: Your audio is processed to generate text transcriptions. See Section 5 for details on third-party API processing.
  • AI Filler Word Removal: Your transcribed text is analyzed by AI to identify and remove filler words (e.g., "um," "uh," "like"). You can review and adjust the results.
  • Multi-Language Transcription: Your selected language preference is used to route transcription to the appropriate language model. We do not use language data for any other purpose.
  • MP3 / MP4 Transcription: Uploaded files are processed for transcription and deleted from our systems once processing is complete, unless you save the transcription yourself.
  • Note Organization: Your folder names, note titles, and metadata are stored to provide the folder organization feature.
  • PDF and Word Export: Notes are converted to PDF or .docx format on-demand. We do not retain copies of exported files.

3.2 App Improvement and Diagnostics

We use crash logs and anonymized diagnostic data to identify and fix bugs. This data never contains content from your recordings or notes.

We will NEVER: sell your data · use your audio to train AI models without your explicit consent · share your recordings with advertisers · access your microphone except when you actively start a recording

Section 04

Data Storage & Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your recordings, transcriptions, notes, and folder structure are stored exclusively on your device. Your content is never uploaded to our servers or any cloud storage. Local data is protected by your device's built-in security (iOS Data Protection, Android Full Disk Encryption).

If you delete the App, locally stored data is removed in accordance with your device OS behavior.

4.2 Temporary Processing Storage

When audio is sent to a third-party transcription API for processing, it may be temporarily stored on that provider's infrastructure. See Section 5 for details on each provider's data handling and retention commitments.

4.4 Security Measures

Since data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security mechanisms. For any data temporarily processed via third-party APIs (e.g., transcription), we ensure it is transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS 1.2 or higher) and deleted after processing. If you discover a security issue, please contact supp.scriptly@gmail.com immediately.

Section 07

Your Privacy Rights

7.1 Rights for All Users

  • Access and Delete Your Content: You can view, edit, and delete all your recordings, transcriptions, and notes directly within the App at any time.
  • Export Your Data: Export your notes and transcriptions as PDF or Word files from within the App.
  • Revoke Microphone Access: Revoke Scriptly's microphone access at any time in your device Settings. This disables recording but does not affect other App functions.
  • Contact Us: Email us at supp.scriptly@gmail.com with any privacy questions or requests.

7.2 EEA and UK Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the EEA or United Kingdom, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right of Access (Art. 15): Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification (Art. 16): Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Erasure (Art. 17): Request deletion of your personal data where we no longer have a lawful basis to retain it.
  • Right to Restriction (Art. 18): Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability (Art. 20): Receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON or CSV).
  • Right to Object (Art. 21): Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7(3)): Withdraw consent at any time where processing is consent-based, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You have the right to contact your local data protection authority. A list of EEA supervisory authorities is available at edpb.europa.eu. UK users may contact the ICO.

We will respond to GDPR requests within 30 days (extendable by 60 days for complex requests with notice).

7.3 California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

California residents have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act and California Privacy Rights Act:

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we collect, use, and share about you.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information we have collected, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing: We do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information: Your voice recordings are Sensitive Personal Information under CPRA. We use this data only to provide the services you request and not to infer characteristics about you.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your rights.

To submit a verifiable consumer request, contact us at supp.scriptly@gmail.com. We will respond within 45 days (extendable by 45 additional days with notice).

Section 10

AI Processing Transparency

Scriptly uses artificial intelligence to power several features. We believe in transparency about how AI interacts with your data.

10.1 Speech-to-Text Transcription

Your audio is processed by an AI model to convert speech to text. This AI performs only the mechanical task of transcription — it does not make decisions about you, assess your emotions or personality, or build a persistent profile of your voice.

10.2 Filler Word Removal

Our AI analyzes your transcribed text to identify and flag common filler words and phrases (e.g., "um," "uh," "like," "you know"). This processing:

  • Produces suggestions that you can review and accept or reject.
  • Does not profile you or build a model of your speech patterns beyond the immediate session.
  • Does not make any consequential decisions about you.

10.3 No AI Training on Your Data

We do not use your audio recordings, transcriptions, or notes to train, fine-tune, or improve any AI model — ours or any third party's — without your separate, explicit, opt-in consent. Our agreements with third-party AI API providers contractually prohibit them from using your data for model training.

10.4 Multi-Language Support

Language detection and multi-language transcription is handled based on your explicit language selection in the App. No language profiling is conducted beyond routing your audio to the appropriate language model.
